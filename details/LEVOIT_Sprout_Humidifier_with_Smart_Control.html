LEVOIT Sprout Humidifier with Smart Control

USD103.49
LEVOIT Sprout Humidifier uses evaporative technology to add moisture while keeping air clean. Features include dual filters, standalone sensor, Auto-Dry, and smart app control. Perfect for baby’s room with a warm nightlight and 36-hour runtime. Easy to use and maintain for a healthy, comfortable environment.
  • Description
  • Reviews

  The LEVOIT Sprout Humidifier is designed with advanced evaporative technology to deliver pure, mist-free moisture to your baby’s room. Its dual air and water filters effectively trap impurities with a diameter of 0.5 microns or larger, ensuring the air remains clean and fresh. The standalone sensor monitors humidity levels directly at the intended location, offering more precise control than traditional humidifiers. With an Auto-Dry function, the wick filter remains dry after use, reducing the risk of mold and supporting a healthier environment. The sleek, warm-toned nightlight creates a calming ambiance, perfect for bedtime relaxation without the harsh blue light.
  Ideal for parents looking to maintain optimal humidity and air quality for their baby, this humidifier offers smart control through the VeSync App. You can set customized humidity levels, receive filter, water, and cleaning reminders, and even adjust the nightlight from your smartphone. Its 3.8L tank ensures up to 36 hours of continuous operation, making it perfect for overnight use. The dishwasher-safe design simplifies maintenance, while its compact and quiet operation fits seamlessly into any nursery setup.
